Asteroids

Asteroids is a game that I have created during the Certificate II course at AIE during our first week of the course.
The overall goal in the game is to destroy as many asteroids as possible in order to receive the highest possible score.

Platformer

This platforming game was created during the second week of the CERTII course, it demonstrates my understanding of cell-based collision and core structure when making a game of the platforming genre.

Group Project Cert II

This game was created with a small team of 4 members during the final week of the certificate II course. We created an RPG style arena game in which you would need to fight monsters in order to earn gold and buy new weapons to fight the stronger enemies that would spawn later in the game.
